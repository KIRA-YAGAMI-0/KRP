Welcome to Karan Raj Palace

Discover paradise in the heart of Kalaiya,Nepal at Karan Raj Palace

Welcome to Karan Raj Palace, a luxurious heritage hotel that offers a glimpse into the grandeur and elegance of India's royal past. Nestled amidst sprawling gardens and located in the heart of the city, our palace hotel provides a tranquil retreat for travelers seeking to escape the hustle and bustle of modern life. Our rooms are spacious, well-appointed, and decorated in a traditional style that evokes the charm and character of India's bygone era. Experience the true essence of royalty with us and be swept away by the opulence and hospitality of Karan Raj Palace.
